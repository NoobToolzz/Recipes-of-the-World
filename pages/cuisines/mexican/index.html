Guacamole

The secret to the best guacamole recipe? Starting with a paste made with coriander/cilantro, chilli and onion. It's the traditional Mexican way. First timers can never pick why it tastes so good, they just know it is waaaay better than the usual!

×

Guacamole Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 small red onion, finely diced
  • 1-2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Prepare Avocados

  1. Cut avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

Mash Avocados

  1. Mash the avocados with a fork or potato masher until you achieve your desired consistency.

Add Ingredients

  1. Add finely diced red onion, diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, and minced garlic to the mashed avocados.

Mix Well

  1. Gently mix all the ingredients until well combined.

Adjust Flavor

  1. Taste the guacamole and adjust the lime, salt, or pepper as needed.

Garnish & Serve

  1. Transfer the guacamole to a serving bowl.
  2. Optionally, garnish with additional cilantro.

Enjoy your fresh and delicious homemade Guacamole! Adjust the ingredients to suit your taste preferences.

Chicken Enchiladas

Chicken Enchiladas made from scratch - juicy, cheesy and full of flavour! There's a few components to homemade enchiladas, but it is absolutely worth the effort with flavour you can never achieve with packet spice mixes and bottled enchilada sauce.

×

Chicken Enchiladas Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 cups shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 8-10 flour or corn tortillas
  • 2 cups enchilada sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and sliced green onions for garnish

Prepare Filling

  1. In a pan, sauté chopped onion and bell pepper until softened.
  2. Add shredded chicken, black beans, corn, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well and cook until heated through.

Assemble Enchiladas

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Warm tortillas according to package instructions.
  3. Spoon the chicken mixture onto each tortilla, roll them up, and place them seam-side down in a baking dish.

Add Sauce and Cheese

  1. Pour enchilada sauce over the rolled tortillas.
  2. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top.

Bake

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Garnish and Serve

  1. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
  2. Garnish with fresh cilantro and sliced green onions.
  3. Serve warm, and optionally, with sour cream or guacamole on the side.

Enjoy your flavorful and cheesy Chicken Enchiladas! Customize with your favorite toppings and sides.

Quesadillas

Quesadillas! Who doesn't go mad over crispy tortillas stuffed with molten, juicy goodness? This is my definitive Quesadilla guide - choose from Chicken Quesadillas, Beef OR Vegetable. Or build your own using the all-purpose Quesadilla seasoning!

×

Quesadillas Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend)
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Optional toppings: salsa, sour cream, guacamole
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Prepare Filling

  1. In a bowl, combine shredded chicken, shredded cheese, diced bell peppers, diced red onion, ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.

Assemble Quesadillas

  1. Lay out the tortillas and evenly distribute the chicken and cheese mixture on one half of each tortilla.

Fold and Press

  1. Fold the tortillas in half, creating a half-moon shape.
  2. Press down gently to ensure the filling sticks together.

Cook Quesadillas

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place quesadillas in the skillet and cook until the tortillas are golden brown and the cheese is melted (about 3-4 minutes per side).

Slice and Serve

  1. Remove from the skillet and let them cool for a moment.
  2. Slice each quesadilla into wedges.

Enjoy your delicious and easy Chicken Quesadillas! Feel free to customize the filling with additional ingredients such as black beans, corn, or jalapeños.

Chicken Chilaquiles

Chilaquiles are basically corn tortilla pieces that are fried, cooked in salsa, and sprinkled with cheese. They are often served for breakfast with eggs and a side of beans or nopalitos.

×

Chicken Chilaquiles Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed or diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jalapeño, seeded and chopped (optional for spice)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tortilla chips
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend)
  • 2 eggs (optional)
  • Samosa wrappers or pastry sheets
  • Fresh cilantro, diced avocado, and sour cream for garnish

Prepare Sauce

  1. In a saucepan, combine crushed tomatoes, chopped onion, minced garlic, jalapeño (if using), ground cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Simmer for 10-15 minutes until flavors meld.

Add Chicken

  1. Stir in shredded chicken and cook until heated through.

Assemble Chilaquiles

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a baking dish, layer tortilla chips followed by the chicken and tomato sauce mixture. Repeat the layers.

Top with Cheese

  1. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top of the layers.

Bake

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Optional Eggs

  1. If desired, crack eggs on top of the chilaquiles during the last 5 minutes of baking. Bake until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny.

Garnish and Serve

  1. Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh cilantro and diced avocado.
  2. Serve hot with a dollop of sour cream on top.

Enjoy your flavorful Chicken Chilaquiles! Customize with your favorite toppings and adjust spice levels to your preference.

Carnitas

Every tortilla dreams of being stuffed with Carnitas. Picture seasoned pork slow-cooked into tender submission, gently shredded and pan-fried to golden, crispy perfection. Carnitas has that elusive combination of juicy and crispy that's so irresistible. The best part of this Carnitas? 5 minutes prep!

×

Carnitas Recipe

Ingredients

  1. 2-3 lbs pork shoulder or pork butt, cut into chunks
  2. 1 onion, quartered
  3. 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 1 orange, juiced
  5. 1 lime, juiced
  6. 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  7. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  8. 1 teaspoon chili powder
  9. Salt and pepper to taste
  10. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Marinate Pork

  1. In a large bowl, combine pork chunks, minced garlic, orange juice, lime juice, ground cumin, dried oregano, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well to ensure the pork is coated. Marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.

Cook Carnitas

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large, oven-safe pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the marinated pork chunks and sear on all sides until browned.

Add Aromatics

  1. Add quartered onion to the pot for added flavor.

Slow Cook

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. Cover the pot and transfer it to the preheated oven.
  3. Slow-cook for 2.5 to 3 hours, or until the pork is tender and easily shreds.

Shred and Crisp

  1. Once the pork is tender, remove it from the pot and shred with two forks.
  2. If desired, crisp the shredded pork by spreading it on a baking sheet and broiling for a few minutes until the edges are golden and crispy.

Serve

  1. Serve the carnitas in tacos, burritos, or over rice.
  2. Garnish with fresh cilantro, diced onions, and a squeeze of lime.

Enjoy your flavorful and tender Carnitas! Customize your toppings and pair with your favorite sides for a delicious meal.
